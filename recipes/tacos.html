Delicious Tacos

These tacos are a flavorful and satisfying meal that's perfect for any occasion. The tender meat is seasoned to perfection and served in warm tortillas with fresh vegetables and a zesty salsa.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat the ground beef in a large pan over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Fill each tortilla with the seasoned beef.
  5. Topp the tacos with lettuce, tomatoes, onion, sour cream, and cheese.
